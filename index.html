About Me

Hello, my name is Justin Committe. I began in the Web Design and Development Campus program in late 2011. I had to return to Atlanta about two-thirds of the way through due to family health problems. I am originally from Jacksonville Florida, but my family moved to Atlanta when I was only one year old. I am thirty-two years old and have been interested in creating things my whole life. I have been making a living doing home renovation work since I graduated from high school in 2001. With Web Development I have the ability to be creative and then to build my creations. I built a website for my father at the beginning of this year for his real estate development company.
