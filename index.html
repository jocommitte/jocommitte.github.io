About Me

Hello, my name is Justin Committe. I began in the Web Design and Development Campus program in late 2011. I had to return to Atlanta about two-thirds of the way through due to family health problems. I am originally from Jacksonville Florida, but my family moved to Atlanta when I was only one year old. I am thirty-two years old and have been interested in creating things my whole life. I have been making a living doing home renovation work since I graduated from high school in 2001. With Web Development I have the ability to be creative and then to build my creations. I built a website for my father at the beginning of this year for his real estate development company. Other than that the only website I have build was my own personal website, which I worked on in 2012 and not much since. Check em out!!

Header

Target Audience

The target audience here is women from the ages of 40-65. Or gay men from the age of 24-50. The income of this target audience is going to be fairly high because French Bulldogs are one of the most expensive kind of dogs to buy. They are small and cute and even though they are too big to go in a purse, which younger women love to do, they are small enough to be enticing to these women and men. They probably live in urban environments and are fairly social in their lives.

Client Goals | User Needs

The goal of the client is to portray information about peoples pets. They do this by showcasing certain vets. The vets are able to advertise on the site. Advertising is how this site gets their money. They bring the user in and then they give them options on how to accomplish their goals, which in the end are taking good care of their dogs. The user is searching for information about how they can be better pet owners. The fine line here is giving enough information that the user is satisfied with the reliability of information, while at the same time encouraging the process of hooking up with one of the vets on the site. They also advertise for groomers and pet carriers. "I trust this site, so I'm going to use their people."

Design Critique

This is a veterinary website and they want you to feel safe about leaving your pet with them. They chose the color green for the background color because it symbolizes safety, harmony, growth. Green has a healing power and what better feeling to get when you're looking for a hospital for your pet than healing? It's a good thing that they stayed away from yellow-green because it puts forth a feeling of sickness and jealousy. They used a kind of childish serif font which manages to put forth a feeling of reliability, while at the same time making them seem approachable. Then there is a picture of the animals they work with. The picture is a cute image of some French Bulldog puppies which continues to tell you who they are and grabs your attention.

CTA

Target Audience

This is a photography site. The target audience here men from the ages of 25-50. The main target audience is men going through their mid-life crisis. Looking for something fun to liven up their lives and make them feel young again. These are men in an urban environment with enough money to understand the high end things in life. One of the pictures on the slide show is a young attractive woman, which is used to entice the men. I'd say the range of income would be between 25,000 a year and 80,000 a year.

Client Goals | User Needs

The client goals are to create an artistic approach to photography and get you to "read more" information they have to showcase. This is basically a showcase site with tons of photography with a well placed geometric design. They want you to fall in love with their photographs in order to make you think that it is not the content of the picture but the way it was taken. This meshes well with the user goals, which are to bring their memories to life in the most stylish way possible. The user wants a clean and crisp picture of their past, so that is how the client is portraying their photographs.

Design Critique

The call to action is clean and crisp. They have an image behind the CTA which portrays the premium of their work. A sans-serif font was used which promotes a clean, modern and friendly attitude. The custom font used for the beginning of the CTA shows a creative approach to a modern way of life. The color orange combines the energy of red and the happiness of yellow which really brings to life the way the user would like to feel when they are looking at their photos. The read more button starts out as an outline, but when you hover over it there is an animation that turns it into yellow. This provokes a coming to life feeling which again makes you feel like the client can make your photos come to life.

Photo Gallery

Target Audience

This is a Travel Site. The target audience is people with enough money to travel. They are middle to upper-class families. Couples from the ages of 40-60 years old. audience is adventurous and does not mind being frivolous with their money. These are people that have lived a working life and been successful at their jobs. They are from a suburban environment and make an annual income of between $70,000 and up. These people have fast internet speeds and have the capability of surfing images with no problem.

Client Goals | User Needs

The goals of the client are to entice the user into using their travel guides. They accomplish this with good photography. Information is provided for the images and when clicked the image blows up. The user goal is information. When the user is traveling they want to be guided through the country in an informative matter. Brand identity is important to this client.

Design Critique

This photo gallery is begun with an "upstanding" serif font which tells me that they want to be trusted with their information. These photos are reliable, these photos are real and not photo-shopped. They then continue with a modern sans-serif font which brings in the feeling that these people will help you get your objective accomplished. They will guide you through the country and describe what you are seeing and make you feel stable while they do it. The pictures are very beautiful and surrounded by a white border. This white border conveys purity and a successful beginning. "This trip will be a success if you go with us.", is the feeling I get from them.
